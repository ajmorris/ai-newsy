Applied Computing wants to give oil and gas operators an AI model for the entire plant

Applied Computing, a London-based startup founded in 2023, has raised a $20 million Series A led by engineering giant KBR with Databricks Ventures participating. The company is building a foundation AI model called Orbital for the oil, gas, and petrochemical industry. Orbital combines time series, physics-based, and language models to predict facility states by analyzing sensor readings, physics/chemistry constraints, and equipment parameters in real time. The startup claims facilities currently use less than 8% of available data for operating decisions, and Orbital can compress investigations from days or weeks into seconds. Applied Computing reports reaching double-digit millions in annual recurring revenue within 18 months of leaving stealth.
